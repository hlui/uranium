Toggler

What the widget does

TOGGLER

Click on the button

and the content appears

What to put in your HTML

<div data-ur-set="toggler">
  <p>TOGGLER</p>
  <div data-ur-toggler-component="button">
    <p>Click on the button</p>
  </div>
  <div data-ur-toggler-component="content">
    <p>and the content appears</p>
  </div>
</div>

Select from a List

What the widget does

CLICK ON AN ITEM TO SELECT

  • A
  • B
  • C
  • D

What to put in your HTML

<div data-ur-set='select-list'>
  <select data-ur-select-list-component='select'>
    <option selected='true' value='SKU-A'> A </option>
    <option value='SKU-B'> B </option>
    <option value='SKU-C'> C </option>
    <option value='SKU-D'> D </option>
  </select>
  <ul data-ur-select-list-component='content'>
    <li value='SKU-A'> A </li>
    <li value='SKU-B'> B </li>
    <li value='SKU-C'> C </li>
    <li value='SKU-D'> D </li>
  <ul>
</div>
