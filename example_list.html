Examples

But wait! There's more...

On the basic widget pages, we've included the simplest (in our mind) examples of the widgets. But there's so much more you can do with these guys.

In these example pages we've included some other ways in which you can use the widgets. For example, rather than a toggler just being an accordion (as we saw in its widget page), we can make it into a pop-up.

Even though there's a fair few of them here, the examples aren't exhaustive. The flexibility of the widgets means that it's only really down to the imaginability with your CSS!
